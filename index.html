Our Story

Crafted with Love,
Served with Soul

Brewed Awakening was born from a simple belief: that great coffee can transform ordinary moments into extraordinary ones. Founded in 2019, we've grown from a tiny corner shop into a beloved community gathering place.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasting them in small batches to ensure every cup delivers exceptional flavor and aroma.
